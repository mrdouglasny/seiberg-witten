# Archived audit documents

Completed campaign plans, superseded scoping documents, one-shot review artifacts, and
the original jacobian-challenge phase plan are kept **locally** in the gitignored
`history/` directory (`history/audit/`, `history/docs/`) and are not on the remote.
They are logs of finished work; the current state they produced is recorded in the
living documents here (`PROOF_STATUS.md`, `FAITHFULNESS.md`, `REVIEWER.md`,
`DIFFICULT_POINTS.md`) and in the paper. Lean module headers that cite a
`audit/*_PLAN.md` by name refer to these archived files.

Living planning documents remaining in `audit/`: `PERIOD_LAYER_DISCHARGE.md` (the
roadmap for the one remaining coarse axiom, `periodRigidityAxiom`) and
`RIEMANN_PERIODS_SCOPE.md` (the scope of the `RiemannPeriods` library).
